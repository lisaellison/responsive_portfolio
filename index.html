About me

Lisa is a classics instructor at East Carolina University where she regularly teaches beginning & intermediate Latin and the literature of ancient Greece & Rome in translation. She serves on several committees and maintains the webpages for two small multi-disciplinary programs: Classical Studies and Great Books.

Lisa started programming when she was a child, creating games using BASIC under her father's tutelage. She has remained active in the world of technology since.

In her free time, Lisa enjoys baking, taking her cats for "walks" and reading.

It's Lisa's goal to use artfully crafted technology to help bring classics and the humanities to more humans as a useful framework for thinking about who we are and what we're doing here.
