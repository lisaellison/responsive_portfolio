About me

Lisa is a classics instructor at East Carolina University where she regularly teaches beginning & intermediate Latin and the literature of ancient Greece & Rome in translation. She serves on several committees and maintains the webpages for two small multi-disciplinary programs: Classical Studies and Great Books.

Lisa wrote her first programs as a child, creating games using BASIC on an Atari computer her dad enjoyed taking apart & putting back together again on the kitchen table. Though she hasn't been actively programming since then, she has remained an active user of technology.

In her free time, Lisa enjoys baking bread. She does not like eating it all the much though. If you're curious to know what sorts of things she reads, check out her Goodreads profile.

It's Lisa's goal to use artfully crafted programs to bring classics and the humanities to more humans.
